The Challenge

Celesta: An iGaming Platform With No Organic Presence

Celesta (celesta.io) is a B2B iGaming software platform offering game aggregation, turnkey casino solutions, and sports betting infrastructure to operators across multiple markets. Despite a strong product and a growing commercial footprint, the website had almost no organic search visibility.

At the start of the engagement in early 2025, Celesta was ranking for only 18 keywords, averaging position 38.7, with just 6 terms in the Top 10. No structured SEO layer existed. No content strategy. No internal linking architecture.

The goal: establish genuine search authority in a competitive, regulated niche and drive qualified organic traffic from operators actively searching for iGaming technology solutions.

The Strategy

What We Built — and Why It Worked

A structured, phased approach covering every layer of SEO: technical, on-page, content, and internal linking.

01

Technical SEO Foundation

Conducted a full technical audit across all indexable URLs on celesta.io. Fixed crawlability issues, optimized canonical structure, resolved duplicate content signals, and ensured clean rendering across the Strapi-based CMS. Established proper HTTP status codes, sitemap integrity, and structured data scaffolding.

Crawl auditCanonical fixesStructured data (Schema)Sitemap & robots.txtCore Web Vitals
02

Keyword Research & SEO Layer

Mapped a full keyword universe across all Celesta solution verticals: game aggregation, iGaming platform, sports betting software, turnkey casino, and hosting. Assigned primary and secondary keywords to each page with zero cannibalization. Delivered optimized titles, meta descriptions, H1s, H2 structures, and GEO-focused schema per page.

Competitive keyword gap analysisPer-page SEO layerKeyword cannibalization checkGEO / AI search optimization
03

Content Strategy & New Page Creation

Identified topical gaps where competitors were ranking but Celesta had no page. Built new solution pages targeting high-intent iGaming queries — including game aggregator, crypto casino, sports betting software, and turnkey solution pages. Developed a blog content calendar targeting commercially adjacent keywords to broaden topical authority.

New solution pagesBlog content calendarTopical authority mappingCommercial + informational mix
04

Internal Linking Architecture

Rebuilt celesta.io's internal link structure from scratch. Created a systematic linking plan connecting solution pages, blog content, and the homepage — passing authority to the highest-priority commercial URLs with diverse, contextually relevant anchor text.

Priority page link flowAnchor text strategyBlog-to-solution links
05

Tracking & Reporting Automation

Implemented automated keyword rank tracking via n8n and Google Search Console integration for celesta.io. Built a live reporting dashboard in Google Sheets tracking keyword movements, page-level performance, and impression/click trends — updated weekly for continuous optimization decisions.
